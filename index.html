LOCATION: Southeastern Tennessee

DOSSIER #3: BLUE RIDGE

Georgia

LOCATION: North Georgia Mountains

DESCRIPTION: Charming mountain town with antique shops, local wineries, and scenic mountain views. Perfect for relaxation with a touch of adventure.

ACTIVITIES: Blue Ridge Scenic Railway, hiking trails, downtown shopping, wine tasting, waterfall hikes, cabin stays with mountain views

BEST VALUE SEASON: Late November-early December or mid-March to April (post-fall colors, pre-summer, better rates)

DOSSIER #4: MENTONE

Lookout Mountain, Alabama

LOCATION: Lookout Mountain plateau, Alabama

DESCRIPTION: Quiet mountain retreat with stunning views, waterfalls, and local charm. Alabama's hidden gem for peaceful getaways.

ACTIVITIES: DeSoto Falls, Little River Canyon, downtown Mentone shops, hiking, scenic drives, cozy cabin dining

BEST VALUE SEASON: March-April or November (beautiful weather, minimal crowds, best cabin deals)

DOSSIER #5: FAIRHOPE

Alabama

LOCATION: Eastern Shore of Mobile Bay

DESCRIPTION: Artistic coastal town with tree-lined streets, bay views, and exceptional restaurants. Perfect blend of art, food, and relaxation.

ACTIVITIES: Downtown art galleries, Fairhope Pier, boutique shopping, bay sunsets, farm-to-table dining, nearby beaches

BEST VALUE SEASON: September-October or March-April (pleasant weather, fewer tourists, better rates)
